Email partnerships@ensafrica.foundation or use the form above with subject "partnership" — our team responds within 48 hours.

Absolutely. Write to probono@ensafrica.foundation and tell us about your expertise. We welcome practitioners at all levels.
Scholarship inquiries
For the Future Lawyers Programme, contact future@ensafrica.foundation — applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
