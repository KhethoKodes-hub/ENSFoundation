Common Questions

I want to partner or sponsor
Email partnerships@ensafrica.foundation or use the contact form above. We love new collaborations that create lasting impact.
I'm a lawyer — can I volunteer?
Absolutely. Write to probono@ensafrica.foundation. Your skills can change lives through our Access to Justice programme.
Scholarship inquiries
For the Future Lawyers Programme, contact future@ensafrica.foundation. We support Africa's next generation of legal changemakers.
Media & press
Our communications team is available at press@ensafrica.foundation. We welcome coverage that amplifies our mission.
