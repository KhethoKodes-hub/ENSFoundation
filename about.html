02
Origin Story

From ENSafrica's
century of legacy

ENSafrica has advised across the continent for over a century. In 2022, partners and practitioners formalised a shared vision: a foundation that channels the firm's legal expertise into direct social investment.

What began as small pro bono networks in Johannesburg, Nairobi and Accra quickly grew into a structured non-profit. Today we operate with dedicated staff, a board drawn from the legal community, and projects in 14 countries.

Our name carries the reputation of Africa's largest law firm — but our heart beats for the communities behind the cases.
